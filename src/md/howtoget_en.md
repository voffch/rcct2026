### Conference Venue
The RCCT-2026 conference will be held at the Ural Federal University, Institute of Natural Sciences and Mathematics, 48 Kuibysheva Street, Ekaterinburg. (see <a class="link underline" target="_blank" href="https://yandex.ru/maps/?um=constructor%3A8af9ff7be3c7e51377dec9cf4b284e533823c5ef13eb6fc56df491ceec13d099">Yandex Maps</a>).

<i>door_front</i> The main entrance to the building is located on Kuibysheva Street.

##### <i>directions_bus</i> Buses and trolleybuses
*From the city center (to the south)*: buses № 60, 77, 86 or trolleybuses № 27, 31, 35, 36.
- Stop: "Сандуновские бани", "Sandunovskie Bani" (on Rosa Luxemburg St.).  
Walking distance to the entrance: ~5 minutes.

*To the center (north)*: buses #60, 65, 77, 89 or trolleybuses #27, 31, 35, 36.
- Stop: "Улица Куйбышева", "Ulitsa Kuibysheva" (on Belinskogo St.).  
Walking distance to the entrance: ~5 minutes.

##### <i>tram</i> Trams
- Stop: "Дом художника", "Dom Khudozhnika" (trams № 3, 6, 10, 20, 21).  
Walking distance to the entrance: ~8 minutes.

##### <i>directions_run</i> Walking from the subway and other stops
- From subway station "Geologicheskaya" ("Геологическая"), "Tsirk" ("Цирк") stop or "TRTS Grinvich" ("ТРЦ Гринвич") stop  
Walking distance to the entrance: ~20 minutes. Please note that the section of Kuibysheva Street (from 8 Marta St. to Gorky St.) is currently completely CLOSED TO ALL TRANSPORT AND PEDESTRIANS (see <a class="link underline" target="_blank" href="https://yandex.ru/maps/?um=constructor%3A8af9ff7be3c7e51377dec9cf4b284e533823c5ef13eb6fc56df491ceec13d099">walking directions</a> on the map).

<i>exclamation</i> To enter the university building, please bring your PASSPORT.
