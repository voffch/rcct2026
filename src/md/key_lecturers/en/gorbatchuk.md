Doctor of Chemical Sciences, Professor in the Department of Physical Chemistry at the A.M. Butlerov Chemical Institute of Kazan Federal University, <a class="link underline" href="https://kpfu.ru/Valery.Gorbatchuk" target="_blank">kpfu.ru/Valery.Gorbatchuk</a>. He graduated from the Chemistry Department of Kazan State University (now Kazan Federal University) in 1979. He defended his PhD thesis in 1982 and his doctoral dissertation in 2003. He specializes in the thermodynamics of intermolecular interactions, molecular recognition, supramolecular chemistry, and the polymorphism of organic substances, including medicinal substances.
